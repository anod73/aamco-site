Asset and Estate Matters

We provide services in connection with residential and commercial properties on a range of issues such as:-

  • Sale and Purchase
  • Leases and tenancies
  • Assignment and vesting of rights in properties
  • Alienation, sub-division, partitioning, amalgamation of title
  • Land development projects
  • Strata titles
  • Applications to relevant authorities in respect of land conversions
  • Real property gains tax
  • Charges, caveats, liens
  • Financing of property purchase and security
  • Forfeiture and compulsory acquisition
